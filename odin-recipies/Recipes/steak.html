Pasta

Steak is universally known as being the best in the world! Follow along for an enjoyable dinner!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Thyme

Instructions

  1. Set skillet to high temp!
  2. after 3 minutes, drop in 1 cup of butter and let it melt
  3. Drop the steak and flip it around after 3 minutes
  4. Once soaked, add thyme above the steak
  5. Remove the stake once satisfied with color
  6. Leave to rest for 10 minutes!
