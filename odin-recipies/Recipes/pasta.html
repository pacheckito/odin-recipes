Pasta

Pasta is universally known as being the best in the world! Follow along for an enjoyable dinner!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • water
  • spaghetti

Instructions

  1. drop flour on table and make a ring down the middle
  2. drop in three egg yolks in the middle and start mixing
  3. After around 10 minutes of kneading, let the dough rest for 1h
  4. Cut the dough into the portions of your choice
  5. cut your favorite pasta shape out with a knife
  6. Cook the pasta for 14 minutes on boiling water
